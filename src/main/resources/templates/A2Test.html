1. Read the four sentences given below and choose the one which uses will or won't incorrectly.

1. Today, many entrepreneurs conduct their business operations online. This method of running business will become very popular in the future.

2. Mike won't be late because he has already taken a taxi.

3. We will move to another city next week because of our boss's job requirements.

4. He won't buy an electric car of new design as he trusts new technological progress in the auto-mobile industry. (true)

2. Read the sentence and add the proper pronoun to make it meaningful. _______were discussing acute global problems when the professor entered the classroom and said to them, “Hello, students!”?

1. he

2. they (true)

3. we

4. I

1. tall (true)

2. long

3. much

4. high
